# Digital Degrowth

A manifesto for dismantling digital colonialism and building a technology stack that serves the people, not the empire.

## Author
Michael Kwet - Researcher and activist focused on digital colonialism and the political economy of the internet.

## Key Concepts
- **Digital Degrowth**: A movement to unlearn obsession with infinite digital expansion and prioritize human-scale infrastructure.
- **Digital Colonialism**: The use of digital technology for political, economic, and social domination.
- **Sovereign Infrastructure**: Decentralized, community-owned technology stacks.
